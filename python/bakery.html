Our Story

Baked with Purpose, Served with Love

SweetCrust Bakery began in a small kitchen in 2012, born from a simple belief — that the best food comes from honest ingredients and unhurried craft. Today, we bake over 40 varieties of pastries, cakes, and breads daily.

Every recipe is tested meticulously. Every ingredient is sourced with care. From our stone-milled flour to our single-origin chocolate — quality is never compromised.

Who We Are

A Labour of Love Since 2012

SweetCrust Bakery was founded by Sophie Laurent, a Paris-trained pastry chef who brought her love of French technique to a neighbourhood kitchen. What started as weekend pop-ups became a full-scale artisan bakery beloved by thousands.

We believe in slow baking — no shortcuts, no preservatives, no compromises. Everything you taste has been prepared with patience and precision.
